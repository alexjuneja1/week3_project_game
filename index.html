Press It!

Press It! is a game that tests fast reflexes as well as keyboard knowledge. Press the keys when you see them on the screen. But be careful, the time will decrease with each keystroke! How far can you go?

Keys used (All lowercase!):

  • A
  • S
  • D
  • F
  • G
  • H
  • J
  • K
  • L

    • Hit the Space bar to start the Game!
